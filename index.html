About Me

Hi! My name is Roy Frias It is a pleasure to meet you.
I am a Freelance Shopify Developer who is here to help you achieve your goals and expectations from your Ecommerce website. I would love to work with you to help you solve and design beautiful websites for all of your business needs.

my services

Shopify Store Migrations

I provide store migrations from other platforms to Shopify. I can migrate your product data, theme templates and all content from your site.

Store Setup

I will deliver a seamlessly store set up. I can set up any Shopify themes you purchase and build collections, set up navigation for your site and filters.

Theme Development

I can create a brand new site for you, using any Shopify theme as well as design any features you want with any theme. We can use a new theme or your previous theme.

App Integration

I can help you integrate Apps for your site, such as language translator or even a Currency converter.

Store owner training

I'll train you with Shopify knowledge in order for you to make any changes or edits to your own store, giving you full control of any changes you make.

Site Speed Optimization

I can help with improving your site speed and loading times. I can also assist with you SEO and improve your online search results.
